My Beloved

Like petals kissed by morning light,
My love for you blooms pure and bright.
Each glance you give, each word you say,
Paints my world in a gentler way.

Your touch has stirred a sacred fire,
A quiet ache, a deep desire.
No verse could hold what my heart knew—
The soul-deep peace I find in you.

And now, amidst the laughter and sound,
In this warm, familiar space we've found,
I turn to you with heart laid bare—
Would you let me be the one who's always there?

To walk beside you, hand in hand,
Through shifting tides and changing sand.
To share our dreams, both big and small,
To build a love that dares not fall.

Forever yours, if you'll allow,
Not just for someday—but for now.
All tomorrows, side by side,
With endless love I cannot hide.
